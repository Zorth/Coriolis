Her goal is to gain fame and recognition.

# History
Born and grew up in [[Kreturu]], mix of private and corporate education. [[Dutsh]] family got involved with the [[Vultures]] gang.
Moved to [[Black Moonlight]] to start her [[Rockerboy]] career, and to separate herself from her family.
